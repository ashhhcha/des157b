Usability Results

Three observations I noted with my participant:

was hesitant where to go after second page

The instructions were a bit unclear at first, and he was clicking around to move the page.

wished the visual design was most stimulating

He wants to see more shapes that suggest the environment or landscape the user is in.

wasn't sure how far to scroll down

He was unsure how far down he had to keep scrolling and wished to see a visual indicator of how much more he has to keep going.

In response to this feedback, I will create a notification at the side of the screen indicating to the user to scroll down whenever the page changes. I am also considering a small progress bar at the top of the screen to show the user how much of the page they've completed. I will also change the background of the last page to a desert landscape to help the user visualize the environment.
