People need to be more cautious about more viruses spreading. With the emergence of new viruses, there is a possibility to wear masks in public again, and have more lockdowns based on the severity of symptoms because we don't have vaccines for them yet. It doesn't take much jumping from animal to animal for it to infect humans.

Almeroth-Williams, Tom. “New Book Shows How to Build a More Flood Resilient Future.” University of Cambridge, 4 Feb. 2020, www.cam.ac.uk/stories/building-a-flood-resilient-future.

  • As there is more flooding with climate change, architects need to start building homes and cities differently. There are six approaches to protection from a flood: attenuate, alleviate, restrict, create, realign, and embrace. To help people return to normalcy after a flood, raising the architecture can help flow the water out faster. Walkways may be raised into bridges to stay above higher water levels, creating a path for people to walk when there is flooding. The way we navigate a city, and use everyday appliances in our homes will have shifts.
